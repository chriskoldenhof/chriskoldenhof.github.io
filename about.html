About Me

This is what I do.

Oh, hello. Umm... I didn't think people were ever really interested in reading about the author.

Well, if you're still here, I guess I can tell you a little bit about myself. Originally from Canada, I've been living in Japan for the last 10+ years. Back when I was a kid, there was a rule in my house; if you didn't cook, you have to clean. It was my aversion to doing dishes that eventually led me to learn how to prepare food. By the time I was a university student I was working part-time as a line cook at a family restaurant where I picked up a lot of basics and started experimenting with different kinds of dishes.

I've spent a lot of time living in small apartments during my time in Japan, so I had to get creative with the little space I had at my disposal. I also didn't want to devote too much time to preparation, so I tend to look for quick and easy recipes that can be done on the stovetop. Most of the recipes here stem from these experiences. I've since gotten a slightly bigger kitchen and a few new toys to play with, but for the most part these recipes will be quite simple. I hope you enjoy them.

I'm also very open to feedback, so feel free to get in touch. If you have a recipe you'd like to share, or any tweaks you'd like to add, please don't hesitate. I only ask that you keep your comments constructive.
